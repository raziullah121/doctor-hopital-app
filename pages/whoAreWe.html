About Doctors Hospital

The Leading Healthcare Provider in Pakistan

Located in the heart of Lahore, Doctors Hospital & Medical Centre is an internationally renowned 250-bed tertiary care multi-specialty hospital and a healthcare provider. The Hospital is a modern facility with sub-specialized Medical, Surgical, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, and Paediatric departments complemented by state-of-the-art anesthesia, radiological, and laboratory services.

DHMC first started its services in the year 2000, when a group of leading medical specialists based in the United States decided to bring their medical expertise back to their country of origin. What started as an idea in a Pennsylvania retreat later became a beacon of healthcare excellence in Pakistan.

DHMC

In-Service of the People

The primary objective behind the hospital’s inception and initiation of services was and always has been to “serve” our patients and community.

Our initiative runs beyond medicine. We want good health and quality of life for every child, adult, and older adult in Pakistan. DHMC stands for accessible and cost-efficient healthcare services for all Pakistanis. At DHMC, we not only strive for health equality but health equity as well. We believe in equal access to health services to those in equal need of it.

DHMC

Moving Beyond Traditional Healthcare Services

As a pioneer of innovative healthcare practices in Pakistan, Doctors Hospital & Medical Centre (DHMC) has a tradition of providing high-quality medical care to its community while maintaining its prime focus on continuous quality improvement.

DHMC consistently invests in the latest health technology advancements to provide high-quality care to our patients while simultaneously pursuing innovation in our clinical outcomes, infrastructure, and staff development.

With highly experienced and dedicated American & British Board-Certified doctors, well-trained nurses, and skilled paramedical staff, Doctors Hospital & Medical Centre strives to take healthcare to a level of service delivery not seen before in the country.

The hospital aims to become a destination of choice by ensuring high-quality healthcare services through its professionalism, ethical conduct, adherence to high standards of care, patient safety, and an inclusive work environment.
