Executive Team

Doctors Hospital Leadership

Doctors Hospital has a deep commitment to creating a health community with help of world-renowned medical experts, healthcare services, and a drive to achieve medical goals. Our success in achieving the milestones to date has been primarily due to the leadership of the hospital and its unique vision. Our Board of Directors and Executive Management Team are committed to stewardship of comprehensive, compassionate, and all-encompassing healthcare services at the hospital.

Our strategy at Doctors Hospital & Medical Center has always been to create an impact on Pakistan’s healthcare landscape by providing service at par with international standards, that is also accessible for wide strata of the society and cost-efficient.

Dr. Muhammad Sarwar

About Us

Founded in 2000, Doctors Hospital is an internationally renowned 250-bed hospital located in Lahore, Pakistan. For more than 18 years we have been fulfilling the medical needs of our community with our staff of American & British Board Certified specialists.
