DHMC Facilities

Doctors Hospital and Medical Center is known for providing quality medical care to our community with help of the latest technology. Our physicians and surgeons offer compassionate healthcare services to the people of Lahore and beyond.

DHMS facilities also include state-of-the-art diagnostic, imaging, and operating systems that provide safe, prompt, and quality care to the patients.

Emergency Care

Emergency Care

DHMC serves you with excellent healthcare services when you need them and at the most vulnerable moments of your life. We are here to take care of you from anything as mundane as seasonal infections to life-saving emergency surgeries.

Outpatient Services

Outpatient Services

From medical advice, patient education, to continuous health monitoring programs, our competent consultants and physicians make sure that you get the care you deserve.

ICU Facilities

ICU Facilities

Health can be unpredictable. DHMC facilities are known for outstanding Intensive Care Units (ICUs) for patients who need extra help in getting better. With help of the latest technology and innovative tools, the on-call DHMC doctors and nurses in specialized medical units work tirelessly to save lives.

Operating Theaters

Operating Theaters

Our operating theaters are equipped with state-of-the-art and innovative technology to ensure better health outcomes for patients undergoing procedures. From appendectomy to transplants, we ensure superior surgical services and treatments.

Wards and VIP Suites

Wards and VIP Suites

Our comfortable wards and private rooms offer you the right space for recovery and regaining your constitution, under monitored and continuous care. We have spaces to accommodate you according to your preferences. Our private suites come with amenities like attached washrooms, LED screens, a companion rest area, and mini-fridges..

Pediatrics

Pediatrics

At DHMC, we are your partner in health throughout life, starting from birth to adulthood. Our pediatrics team and facilities are here to support you through the most important part of your and your children’s lives. DHMC pediatrics facilities (including wards and neonatal ICUs) come equipped with cutting-edge technology and US board-certified physicians..

Pathology

Pathology

A significant aspect of disease diagnosis is quick and reliable pathology services. DHMC pathology labs run a variety of diagnostic panels and tests using the latest equipment while complying with strict rules of quality control.

Imaging and Nuclear

Imaging and Nuclear Medicine

Our advanced Imaging and Nuclear Medicine wings help medical specialists diagnose diseases with high levels of certainty. From 3d ultrasound, CT scans, MRI, to radioactive guided diagnosis procedures- we have it all under one roof.

Pharmacy

Pharmacy

Doctors Hospital & Medical Center has an in-house pharmacy to fulfill all your prescription needs. The pharmacy carries thousands of brand names and generic medications. Under the care of trained pharmacists and staff, we provide home delivery services for verified prescriptions.

About Us

Founded in 2000, Doctors Hospital is an internationally renowned 250-bed hospital located in Lahore, Pakistan. For more than 18 years we have been fulfilling the medical needs of our community with our staff of American & British Board Certified specialists.
